How to deposit TNBC?

  • To deposit, you'll need to have a wallet installed (Official or Keysign) and have some TNBC in that wallet.

  • Navigate to tnbCrow discord server.

  • Use /deposit tnbc command. You'll be prompted with account number and MEMO.

  • Send TNBC to the account number with MEMO. Warning: MEMO is required, or you might lose your coins.

  • Sent? Click on Sent? Scan Chain button to retrive your latest crow bot balance.


    • How to withdraw TNBC?

  • Set withdrawal address if you have not already with the command /set_withdrawal_address tnbc account_number: ACCOUNT_NUMBER

  • Use /withdraw tnbc amount: AMOUNT command to withdraw amount into your withdrawal address.

    • (Note: Network fees will be deducted automatically from your account with each withdrawal)

    How to escrow TNBC?

  • Once you have a trade partner and agreed deal, use the command /escrow new amount: AMOUNT user: TRADE_PARTNER

  • The amount of tnbc will be escrowed. Now you can ask the buyer to send your preferred payment method and release escrow once you've received the payment.


    • How to release escrow?

    Warning: Never release the escrow before you know that you have received payment from the TNBC buyer.

  • Find and copy the ID of escrow you want to release using /escrow all.

  • Use command /escrow release escrow_id: ESCROW_ID to release escrow.


    • How to handle disputes in trade?

    If there's a dispute while trading, you can take your case to tnbcrow support member and resolve the issue. Once a trade is disputed, only the support member will be able to unlock the TNBC.

  • Find and copy the ID of escrow you want to dispute using /escrow all

  • Use the command /escrow dispute escrow_id: ESCROW_ID to mark the escrow as disputed.

  • One of the tnbcrow support member will create a private channel within the tnbcrow discord server.

  • Have your say in the private group and the support member will resolve the dispute checking the proofs from the both side.

    • Warning: Agent will never direct message you.
